Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 16, 2026

DailyLumen ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the DailyLumen iOS application.

✦

1. Information We Do Not Collect

DailyLumen is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not share any data with third parties

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All data generated by DailyLumen is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Quotes you have saved to your collection
  • Your reading streak and history
  • Notification preferences and settings
  • Your daily quote viewing history

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Local Notifications

DailyLumen may send you local push notifications as a daily reminder to read your quote. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. We do not use remote push notification services, and no data is sent to external servers in connection with notifications.

4. App Store Purchase

DailyLumen is available as a paid app through the Apple App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information such as credit card numbers or billing details. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles purchase data.

5. Children's Privacy

DailyLumen does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is appropriate for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
